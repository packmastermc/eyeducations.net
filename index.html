K12 Support

We specialize in K-12 special education tutoring including reading, writing, math and critical thinking. We adopt the standardized testing system. The courses are different from traditional AP courses or IB courses. Instead, our courses are specifically developed with an orientation and emphasis on a standard intelligence test. Some of our students, even at a junior high school level began to get high scores in GMAT and LSAT exams.
