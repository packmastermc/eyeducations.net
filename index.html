EY EDUCATION

EY Education is a leading K-12 education service provider within the EY Group. Academic achievement is our priority at EY Education. To help our students stay motivated and distinguished, our world-class educational professionals provide exceptional learning experiences and support. Different from traditional tutorial schools, EY Education specializes in special education and homeschool support to K-12 groups. Founded in 2017, EY Education has been partnering with K-12 campuses since 2022.

K12 Support

We specialize in K-12 special education tutoring including reading, writing, math and critical thinking. We adopt the standardized testing system. The courses are different from traditional AP courses or IB courses. Instead, our courses are specifically developed with an orientation and emphasis on a standard intelligence test. Some of our students, even at a junior high school level began to get high scores in GMAT and LSAT exams.
